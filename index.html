Product Care +

Due to the delicate nature of this product we recommend:

  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, oils, chemical substances, makeup, alcoholic or fatty substances, generic cleaning products such as shoe polish, trichloroethylene, stain removers, or solvents.
  • Avoid contact with rough or abrasive surfaces.
  • Do not let your item come into contact with light, humidity, and direct heat for long periods of time.
  • If your product comes into contact with water, gently pat dry. Avoid excessive rubbing.
  • Store in its protective dust bag when not in use, padded with tissue paper to maintain shape.
