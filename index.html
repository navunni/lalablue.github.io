Product Care +

Due to the delicate nature of this product we recommend:

  • Avoid contact with water, perfumes, oils, chemical substances, makeup, alcoholic or fatty substances, generic cleaning products such as shoe polish, trichloroethylene, stain removers, or solvents as discoloration can occur and alter the appearance of your product.
  • Avoid contact with rough or abrasive surfaces, so that your product does not scratch.
  • Avoid contact of dark colored products with light or bright colored products in hot or humid weather to prevent potential transfer of color.
  • Do not let your item come into contact with light, humidity, and direct heat for long periods of time as it may affect the appearance of your product.
  • If your product comes into contact with water, gently pat the area(s) dry. Avoid excessive rubbing.
  • Store the product in its protective dust bag when not in use. To maintain this bag's shape, we recommend padding it with tissue paper.
